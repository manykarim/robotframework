## Why

The 6 main section index pages each have a TOC but no introductory text, leaving readers with no sense of what they're about to enter before they start scanning links. A short, human-voice paragraph above the TOC frames the section's purpose and surfaces the highest-value entry points immediately.

## What Changes

- Each of the 6 section `index.md` files gains a short introductory paragraph (2–4 sentences) placed above the existing TOC.
- The paragraphs are written in the Robot Framework Voice: direct, technically grounded, 70/30 professional/casual, dry humor where appropriate — no corporate fluff.
- Each paragraph links to the 1–3 most important sub-pages within the section.
